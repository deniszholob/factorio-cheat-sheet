Use balancers to distribute items over multiple belts, like at ore patches or on the main bus.

There are four kinds of balancers:

  • Input balanced, take evenly from input belts.
  • Output balanced, distribute evenly to output belts.
  • Output balanced under back pressure, keeps even distribution, even if any output belt is blocked.
  • Throughput limited, may have bottlenecks.

4x4 belt balancer

Tips
  • Only use balancers when needed.
  • If your assembly machines consume a full belt you don't need a balancer.
  • These throughput unlimited balancers cover most cases: 44 and 88.
  • You can find more balancers online, like the Balancer Book by Raynquist.
