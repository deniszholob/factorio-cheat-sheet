Shortcuts (Most used)

  • Alt Show detailed information on entities. Or click the ALT button in the shortcut bar.
  • M Open or close the map view.
  • O Open or close the train view.
  • P Open or close the production view.
  • MMB Clear the item in the quick bar. Or set filters in cargo wagons and your inventory. (CMD+RMB on macOS)
  • R Rotate ghosts (or built entities) clockwise.
  • Shift + R Rotate ghosts (or built entities) counterclockwise.
  • CTRL + LMB Put or take all items.
  • CTRL + RMB Put or take a half stack of items.
  • Z Drop 1 item over the cursor. You can feed one item into a Assembly machine or chest.
  • F Pick up items from the ground or belt.
  • Shift + LMB Build a ghost of the item.
  • Shift + RMB Copy entity configuration. Shift + LMB Paste entity configuration.
    • Copy/pasting the configuration works on inserters, assemblers, requester chests, combinators, etc.
    • Set up requester chests by copy/pasting the assembler config to the requester chest. This requests enough items for 30 seconds of production.
    • Copy/paste the config to multiple entities by dragging the mouse.
    • Copy individual slots (inventory, cargo wagon).
  • Use Q while hovering over anything to select it from your inventory.
    • Use Q on ores to select miners.
  • CTRL + C, CTRL + V, CTRL + X, CTRL + Z, CTRL + Y Copy, paste, cut, undo and redo your builds. (CMD vs CTRL on macOS.)
  • Numpad - or Numpad + Increase or decrease the size of buildable tiles, like landfill, concrete, etc.
  • Shift + RMB Clear Blueprint or Deconstruction planner filter.
  • Shift + LMB Ping the ground and the map.
  • ~ Open the chat/command line.

Commands (These do not disable achievements)

  • /permissions, disable Craft - Useful for Lazy Bastard Achievement .
  • /screenshot [x resolution] [y resolution] [zoom] - Useful for large/HD screenshots.
    (Resolution is optional and defaults to the current window size and zoom is optional and defaults to 1).
  • [item=iron-plate] / [fluid=crude-oil] - part of Rich text, will display as an icon! Useful for blueprints/station names.
    To quickly get a rich text tag for an item: open the console, then Shift+LMB on the item (eg. in your inventory, a chest, etc.)
    A rich text tag for that item appears in the console and can be copied.

Debug Menu (F4 also F5 )

Some useful options to enable:

  • show-fps - FPS/UPS
  • show-multiplayer-statistics - Latency
  • show-tile-grid, show-raw-tile-transitions - Precision construction
  • show-rail-paths - Less likely to get run over
  • show-transport-line-gaps - Identify unsaturated belts
  • show-player-robots - Makes it clear if there are stragglers
  • allow-increased-zoom

Misc

  • Inserters will always place items on the furthest side of the belt, and prioritize taking items from the closest.
  • Placing over ghosts will preserve the recipes.
