Smelting Facts

  • Smelting iron, copper, and stone each take a base 3.5 seconds to finish. Smelting steel takes base 17.5 seconds.
  • Stone Furnaces have a crafting speed of 1. Both steel and electric furnaces have a crafting speed of 2.
  • One furnace turning iron ore to plate can support one furnace turning iron plate to steel.
  • Stone and Steel Furnaces consume 0.0225 coal/second.

Uranium Facts

  • Uranium processing has a 99.3% chance to produce 1 uranium-238 and a 0.7% chance to produce 1 uranium-235.
  • Its recommended save up 40 U-235 to kick off the Enrichment process in order to speed up U-235 production.
