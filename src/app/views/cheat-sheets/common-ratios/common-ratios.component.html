For example: To make rails, the right ratio is 1 iron stick assembler feeding 2 rail assemblers.
Using the level 3 assemblers you can produce 10 rails every second.

Rocket Components

With no productivity modules a rocket needs 1000 of each + the requirements for the satellite.
This is a ratio of 1100:1050:1000 or simplified of 22:21:20 (divide by 50).
As these components all have the same crafting time, this ratio applies.

As the satellite can't get productivity bonus, you need a ratio of ~814:764:714 per rocket (to be precise: 5700:5350:5000 per 7 rockets), which makes it a ratio of 114:107:100.
