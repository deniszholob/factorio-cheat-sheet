Inserter capacity research is used to increase the stack size of the inserters.

The bonus is more effective when moving from stack to stack (chests, wagons, machines) than when moving from stack to belt or belt to stack as inserters will wait to collect a stack full from a belt, but instantly grab a stack from containers.
